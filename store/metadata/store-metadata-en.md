## Short description
BauBook helps dog owners keep track of their dog profile, walks, pack presence, friends and useful local safety reports in the Venezia-Mestre pilot area.

## Long description
BauBook! is a beta app designed to make everyday life with a dog easier: dog profile, smart pack diary, walks, temporary presence, friends, safety alerts and support channels.

The first pilot area is Venezia-Mestre. The app is built around a local community, lightweight interactions and a careful approach to safety. Feedback features help improve the experience before a broader public launch.

Main features:
- dog profile and useful dog information;
- smart pack diary;
- walks and temporary presence;
- pack friends;
- useful safety reports;
- support and account deletion request.
